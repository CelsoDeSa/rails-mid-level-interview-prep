# Ambiguous Feature Story

## Question

Tell me about a time requirements were unclear. How did you proceed?

## Brief Answer

Clarify goals, identify assumptions, ask questions, propose a small plan, validate with stakeholders, and iterate.

## Comprehensive Explanation

A good answer shows you do not freeze under ambiguity. Use STAR:

- Situation: vague request or unclear integration behavior.
- Task: turn ambiguity into implementable requirements.
- Action: ask about users, edge cases, constraints, success criteria, non-functional needs, and trade-offs.
- Result: delivered a smaller safe version, reduced rework, or discovered hidden requirements early.

Mid-level expectation: you should not need every detail spoon-fed, but you should also not silently guess on critical behavior.

Describe how you would clarify requirements for a Google Calendar sync feature.
