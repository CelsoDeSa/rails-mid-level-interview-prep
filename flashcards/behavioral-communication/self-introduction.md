# Self-Introduction

## Question

Tell me about yourself and your experience as a Rails developer.

## Brief Answer

Give a concise Rails-focused summary: years, stack, production work, strongest areas, and what you are looking for.

## Comprehensive Explanation

An interview-ready answer:

> “I’m a Rails-focused developer with experience building and maintaining production web applications using Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, Sidekiq, Hotwire, and external API integrations. I’ve worked on features involving background processing, exports, Stripe/Klaviyo-style integrations, and customer-facing UI improvements. Recently I’ve used AI tools to accelerate development, but I stay responsible for understanding, testing, debugging, and shipping the work. I’m looking for a mid-level Rails role where I can contribute reliably, communicate well with the team/client, and keep growing technically.”

Keep it under 90 seconds. Do not recite the whole resume. Invite follow-up on concrete projects.

## Practice Prompt

Give your self-introduction in 60–90 seconds.
