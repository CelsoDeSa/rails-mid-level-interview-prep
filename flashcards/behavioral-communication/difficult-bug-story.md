# Difficult Bug Story

## Question

Tell me about a difficult bug you solved.

## Brief Answer

Use STAR: describe the bug, impact, investigation, root cause, fix, validation, and lesson learned.

## Comprehensive Explanation

Structure:

- Situation: what was failing and who was affected?
- Task: what did you need to restore or understand?
- Action: logs, reproduction, isolating variables, checking database/API/jobs, writing a test, applying fix.
- Result: bug fixed, regression test added, monitoring improved, or process changed.

Avoid vague answers like “I just debugged it.” Interviewers want to hear your reasoning. Mention how you narrowed the problem, what false leads you ruled out, and how you validated the fix.

## Practice Prompt

Prepare a 2-minute bug story involving Rails, PostgreSQL, Sidekiq, Hotwire, or an external API.
